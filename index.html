(Free 1-on-1 IIT Mentorship Assessment) ✅
  • Talk 1-on-1 with your child about their current journey
  • Understand their strong & weak topics
  • Discuss study habits, focus levels, time availability
  • Teach a small topic live to evaluate real-time understanding
